## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

We also use similar technologies:

- **Local storage / session storage** — browser-based storage that persists data locally on your device.
- **Pixels and tracking scripts** — small pieces of code that track page interactions or load third-party analytics.
- **Analytics SDKs** — scripts that collect aggregated usage data about how visitors interact with our site.

## 5. Your Cookie Choices

### 5.1 Cookie Consent (EEA/UK)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, we will ask for your consent before placing non-essential cookies on your device. You can manage your preferences through our cookie consent banner when you first visit the site.

### 5.2 Browser Controls

You can manage or delete cookies directly through your browser settings. Here's how to access cookie settings in major browsers:

- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
- **Edge:** Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

Note: Disabling all cookies may affect the functionality of this and other websites.

### 5.3 Opt-Out Tools

- **Google Analytics:** [Google Analytics Opt-out Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- **General advertising opt-outs:** [Network Advertising Initiative opt-out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/) | [Digital Advertising Alliance opt-out](https://optout.aboutads.info/)

### 5.4 Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal to websites. Our website does not currently respond to DNT signals in a standardized way, as there is no universally accepted standard for DNT. We will update this position if a standard is adopted.
