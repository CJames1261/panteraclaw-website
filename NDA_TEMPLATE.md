## 1. Purpose

The Parties intend to explore a potential business relationship involving data engineering, AI development (including Agentic AI workflows, dashboards, visualizations, and advanced analytics), database creation, management, and optimization, or related services (the "Purpose"). In connection with this exploration, each Party may disclose to the other certain confidential and proprietary information. This Agreement governs the protection of that information.

---

## 2. Definition of Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party ("Disclosing Party") to the other Party ("Receiving Party"), whether disclosed orally, in writing, electronically, or by any other means, that:

- Is designated as "confidential," "proprietary," "trade secret," or with a similar marking at the time of disclosure; or
- A reasonable person in the industry would understand to be confidential given the nature of the information and context of disclosure.

Confidential Information includes, without limitation: business strategies and plans, financial data, pricing, client and prospect lists, technical architectures, source code, data models, AI systems (including Agentic AI agents, workflows, prompts, and outputs), machine learning methodologies, datasets (including proprietary training data), product roadmaps, personnel information, and trade secrets.[1][2][3][4][6]

---

## 3. Exclusions

Confidential Information does not include information that:

(a) Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party;
(b) Was already known to the Receiving Party prior to disclosure, as evidenced by written records predating the disclosure;
(c) Is independently developed by the Receiving Party without reference to or use of the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information, as evidenced by written records;
(d) Is received from a third party without restriction and without breach of any confidentiality obligation by such third party; or
(e) Is required to be disclosed by applicable law, court order, or government authority — provided the Receiving Party gives the Disclosing Party prompt written notice (to the extent permitted by law) of such requirement and reasonably cooperates with the Disclosing Party's efforts to seek a protective order or other appropriate remedy at the Disclosing Party's expense.[4]

---

## 4. Obligations of the Receiving Party

Each Receiving Party agrees to:

- Hold Confidential Information in strict confidence using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own similar confidential information of a similar nature, but in no event less than a reasonable degree of care.
- Use Confidential Information solely for the Purpose described in Section 1 and not for any other purpose, including without limitation training, fine-tuning, or inputting into any AI systems, machine learning models, or third-party services without the Disclosing Party's prior written consent.
- Not disclose Confidential Information to any third party without the Disclosing Party's prior written consent, except to its employees, contractors, or advisors who: (i) have a strict need to know for the Purpose, (ii) are informed of the confidential nature of the information, and (iii) are bound by written confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement. The Receiving Party is responsible for any breach by such persons.
- Promptly notify the Disclosing Party in writing upon discovering any unauthorized use, disclosure, or loss of Confidential Information, including any potential data spill or exposure via AI agents or systems.
- Not reverse engineer, decompile, disassemble, or otherwise attempt to derive the composition or underlying information of any Confidential Information.
- Implement reasonable security measures to protect Confidential Information, including encryption, access controls, and segregation of sensitive data to maintain trade secret status, especially when using AI systems or Agentic workflows.[1][2][3][4][5][7]

---

## 5. No License or Rights Granted

Nothing in this Agreement grants the Receiving Party any license, ownership interest, right to use, or other rights in or to the Disclosing Party's Confidential Information, intellectual property, technology, trade secrets, or derivatives thereof. All right, title, and interest in Confidential Information remains the exclusive property of the Disclosing Party. The Receiving Party acquires no rights by implication or estoppel.

---

## 6. Term and Duration of Confidentiality Obligations

This Agreement is effective as of the date first written above and remains in effect for **two (2) years** unless terminated earlier by either Party with **30 days** prior written notice.

Confidentiality obligations with respect to any Confidential Information survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **five (5) years** from the date of disclosure, except for trade secrets, which shall remain confidential for as long as they qualify as trade secrets under applicable law (i.e., derive independent economic value from not being generally known and are subject to reasonable efforts to maintain secrecy).[1][3][4][6]

---

## 7. Return or Destruction of Information

Upon the Disclosing Party's written request or upon termination of this Agreement or the Purpose, the Receiving Party will promptly return or, at the Disclosing Party's election, destroy all tangible and intangible materials containing or embodying Confidential Information, including all copies, backups, and derivatives. The Receiving Party will certify compliance in writing within **ten (10) days**. Electronic copies, including those in AI systems, cloud storage, or agent memories, must be permanently deleted and not retained for training or any other purpose. The Receiving Party may retain one archival copy solely for legal compliance or evidentiary purposes, subject to continued confidentiality obligations.

---

## 8. No Obligation to Proceed

This Agreement does not obligate either Party to enter into any further agreement, business relationship, or transaction. Either Party may terminate discussions or this Agreement at any time for any reason, subject to surviving obligations.

---

## 9. Remedies

Each Party acknowledges that any unauthorized use, disclosure, or breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Disclosing Party for which monetary damages alone would be inadequate. Accordingly, the Disclosing Party is entitled to seek immediate injunctive relief, specific performance, or other equitable remedies in any court of competent jurisdiction, in addition to any other remedies available at law, without the necessity of posting a bond or proving actual damages. The breaching Party will reimburse the non-breaching Party for all costs, including reasonable attorneys' fees, incurred in enforcing this Agreement.

---

## 10. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Utah, without regard to its conflict of law principles. Any dispute arising out of or related to this Agreement shall first be addressed through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved within **30 days**, the dispute shall be submitted to binding arbitration in Salt Lake City, Utah, under the American Arbitration Association (AAA) Commercial Arbitration Rules. The arbitrator's award will be final and enforceable in any court of competent jurisdiction. Notwithstanding the foregoing, either Party may seek injunctive relief in a court of competent jurisdiction at any time.

---

## 11. General Provisions

**Entire Agreement.** This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding between the Parties with respect to the subject matter herein and supersedes all prior or contemporaneous agreements, discussions, or representations, whether oral or written.

**Amendment.** This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by authorized representatives of both Parties.

**Severability.** If any provision is held invalid or unenforceable, the remaining provisions will remain in full force and effect. The Parties agree to replace any invalid provision with a valid one approximating the original intent.

**No Waiver.** No failure or delay by a Party to enforce any right will constitute a waiver of that right or any other right.

**Assignment.** Neither Party may assign this Agreement without the other Party's prior written consent, except that Pantera Claw may assign to an affiliate or successor in connection with a merger or sale of assets.

**Counterparts and Electronic Signatures.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, each of which is an original, and all of which together constitute one instrument. Electronic signatures (e.g., DocuSign) and delivery via email or PDF are valid and binding.

**Notices.** All notices must be in writing and delivered via email or certified mail to the addresses provided above.

**Independent Contractors.** The Parties are independent contractors; nothing herein creates a partnership, joint venture, or agency relationship.
