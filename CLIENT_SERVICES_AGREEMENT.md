# PanteraClaw — Master Services Agreement (MSA)

> **Status:** Draft — review with a licensed attorney before use.
> **Version:** 1.0
> **Last Updated:** April 9, 2026

---

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **PanteraClaw** ("Service Provider," "we," or "us") and the client identified in the applicable Statement of Work ("Client," "you," or "your"). This Agreement governs all professional services engagements between the parties.

By signing a Statement of Work that references this Agreement, both parties agree to be bound by these terms.

---

## 1. Definitions

- **"Agreement"** means this Master Services Agreement and all Statements of Work, exhibits, and amendments incorporated herein.
- **"Statement of Work" (SOW)** means a written document executed by both parties describing the specific scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for a given engagement.
- **"Deliverables"** means the work product, code, models, documentation, reports, or other outputs PanteraClaw produces specifically for Client under an SOW.
- **"Background IP"** means any intellectual property, tools, frameworks, libraries, methodologies, or know-how owned or developed by PanteraClaw independently of any engagement.
- **"Client Data"** means any data, content, or materials provided by Client to PanteraClaw for use in performing services.
- **"Change Order"** means a written amendment to an SOW that modifies scope, timeline, or fees, signed by both parties.

---

## 2. Services

### 2.1 Statements of Work

All services are governed by individual Statements of Work. Each SOW must specify:

- Project scope and objectives
- Deliverables and acceptance criteria
- Project timeline and milestones
- Fees and payment schedule
- Any specific IP ownership terms that differ from Section 8 defaults

An SOW is binding only when signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

### 2.2 Service Standards

PanteraClaw will perform services in a professional and workmanlike manner consistent with industry standards. PanteraClaw does not guarantee specific business outcomes. Technical performance targets (e.g., model accuracy, system uptime) must be explicitly defined in the SOW to be binding.

### 2.3 Subcontractors

PanteraClaw may engage subcontractors or independent contractors to assist in delivering services. PanteraClaw remains responsible for the work of any subcontractor and will ensure subcontractors are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in this Agreement.

---

## 3. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

- Designate an authorized project representative with decision-making authority.
- Provide accurate, complete, and timely information, data, feedback, and approvals required for service delivery.
- Ensure that any data or materials provided to PanteraClaw do not infringe third-party rights and comply with applicable law.
- Provide access to systems, infrastructure, or personnel reasonably necessary to perform the services.
- Review and approve deliverables within the timeframes specified in the SOW.

**Impact of delays.** If Client fails to fulfill any of the above responsibilities and it causes a delay in PanteraClaw's performance, the project timeline will be extended by an equivalent period, and additional fees may apply. Such delays do not constitute a breach by PanteraClaw.

---

## 4. Revisions and Scope Changes

### 4.1 Included Revisions

Each SOW will specify the number of revision rounds included. Revisions are defined as corrections to deliverables that do not meet the agreed specifications. Revisions do not include new requirements, design changes, or feature additions.

### 4.2 Scope Changes

Any change to the agreed scope, deliverables, timeline, or budget requires a signed Change Order. PanteraClaw is not obligated to begin work on out-of-scope requests until a Change Order is executed.

### 4.3 Verbal Approvals

Verbal approvals and instructions do not modify this Agreement or any SOW. All modifications must be in writing.

---

## 5. Fees and Payment

### 5.1 Fees

Fees are set forth in each SOW. PanteraClaw's rates are subject to change for new engagements with 30 days' written notice.

### 5.2 Invoicing and Payment

- Invoices are due within **30 days** of the invoice date unless otherwise specified in the SOW.
- Engagements exceeding $5,000 may require a deposit (typically 25–50%) before work begins, as specified in the SOW.
- Milestone-based engagements: payment is due upon milestone completion and Client approval.

### 5.3 Late Payments

Overdue balances accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (18% per annum) or the maximum rate permitted by applicable law, whichever is lower. PanteraClaw reserves the right to pause or withhold deliverables on accounts more than 15 days past due without liability.

### 5.4 Disputed Invoices

Client must notify PanteraClaw in writing of any invoice dispute within **10 days** of receipt. Undisputed portions remain due by the original payment date.

### 5.5 Expenses

Unless otherwise specified, out-of-pocket expenses (e.g., third-party API costs, cloud infrastructure costs, software licenses) incurred on Client's behalf are billed at cost plus a 10% administrative fee, with prior Client approval for expenses exceeding $500.

### 5.6 Non-Refundable Fees

Fees paid for completed work or milestones are non-refundable unless PanteraClaw has materially failed to deliver agreed deliverables.

## 6. AI-Assisted Deliverables

Where deliverables incorporate AI-generated components, machine learning models, or automated systems, the following applies:

- **Disclosure.** PanteraClaw will identify AI-assisted components in the SOW or project documentation.
- **Output variability.** AI outputs are probabilistic and may produce different results given identical inputs. PanteraClaw warrants that AI components meet any performance metrics explicitly defined in the SOW, but not that outputs will be deterministic.
- **Client review obligation.** Client is solely responsible for reviewing, validating, and approving AI-generated outputs before deploying them in business operations, customer-facing products, or regulated contexts.
- **Third-party model dependencies.** Deliverables may depend on third-party AI APIs. Changes to those APIs may affect performance and may require additional engagement to address.
- **Agentic systems.** For autonomous or agent-based systems, Client assumes full operational responsibility upon project acceptance, including monitoring, oversight, and safeguarding. See Section 7 below.

---

## 7. Agentic and Automated Systems

For engagements involving autonomous, agentic, or automated decision-making systems:

- PanteraClaw will document system behavior, limitations, and recommended oversight procedures as part of the deliverables.
- Client must implement appropriate safeguards before production deployment, including but not limited to: human review checkpoints, rate limits, audit logging, and kill-switch mechanisms.
- **Client assumes full liability** for actions taken by agentic systems after formal project acceptance, including unintended actions resulting from edge cases, model drift, adversarial inputs, or integration failures.
- PanteraClaw strongly recommends a staging/testing phase and phased rollout prior to full production deployment.

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Background IP

PanteraClaw's Background IP — including pre-existing frameworks, tools, methodologies, reusable libraries, and know-how — remains the sole property of PanteraClaw. This Agreement does not transfer any ownership rights in Background IP.

**License grant.** PanteraClaw grants Client a non-exclusive, perpetual, royalty-free license to use any Background IP embedded in Deliverables solely for Client's internal business operations.

### 8.2 Custom Deliverables

Unless the SOW explicitly states that a deliverable is being created as a "work-for-hire" with full IP transfer, custom Deliverables are licensed to Client as follows:

- **Default:** PanteraClaw retains ownership; Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive license for internal use.
- **Full transfer:** Available for an additional fee as specified in the SOW. Transfer takes effect only upon receipt of full payment.

### 8.3 Client IP

Client retains full ownership of all Client Data, Client's pre-existing materials, and Client's proprietary information. PanteraClaw acquires no rights in Client IP other than the limited license to use it for service delivery.

### 8.4 Open-Source Components

Deliverables may incorporate open-source software. PanteraClaw will identify material open-source components used. Client is responsible for compliance with applicable open-source license terms.

### 8.5 Portfolio and Case Study Rights

PanteraClaw may reference Client's name and describe the general nature of the engagement (e.g., "built a customer segmentation model for a retail client") in its portfolio, website, and marketing materials, unless Client requests confidentiality in writing. PanteraClaw will not disclose proprietary business details without Client's written consent.

## 9. Confidentiality

### 9.1 Mutual Obligations

Each party agrees to hold the other party's Confidential Information in strict confidence, use it solely to perform or receive services under this Agreement, and not disclose it to any third party without prior written consent — except to employees or subcontractors with a need to know who are bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

### 9.2 Confidential Information

"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one party to the other that is designated as confidential or that a reasonable person would understand to be confidential given the context, including technical specifications, business strategies, pricing, Client Data, and proprietary methodologies.

### 9.3 Exclusions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is publicly available through no fault of the Receiving Party; (b) was known to the Receiving Party before disclosure; (c) is independently developed without reference to Confidential Information; or (d) must be disclosed by law, provided the Receiving Party gives prompt written notice where permitted.

### 9.4 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for **three (3) years**, or indefinitely for trade secrets.

## 12. Limitation of Liability

TO THE MAXIMUM EXTENT PERMITTED BY LAW, PANTERACLAW'S TOTAL LIABILITY FOR ANY CLAIMS ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO AN ENGAGEMENT SHALL NOT EXCEED THE TOTAL FEES PAID BY CLIENT TO PANTERACLAW UNDER THE APPLICABLE SOW IN THE **SIX (6) MONTHS** PRECEDING THE CLAIM.

IN NO EVENT SHALL PANTERACLAW BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS, LOSS OF DATA, OR BUSINESS INTERRUPTION, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.

**Exceptions.** Nothing in this section limits liability for gross negligence, willful misconduct, breach of confidentiality obligations, or any liability that cannot be excluded under applicable law.

## 13. Indemnification

**By Client.** Client agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless PanteraClaw and its officers, employees, and contractors from any claims, damages, and expenses (including reasonable legal fees) arising from: (a) Client's use of Deliverables in violation of this Agreement; (b) Client Data infringing third-party rights; (c) Client's violation of applicable law; or (d) operation of agentic systems after project acceptance.

**By PanteraClaw.** PanteraClaw agrees to indemnify Client from third-party claims that Deliverables (excluding Client-provided content and identified open-source components) infringe third-party intellectual property rights, provided Client promptly notifies PanteraClaw of such claims and cooperates in the defense.

## 14. Term and Termination

### 14.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the date the first SOW is signed and continues until terminated.

### 14.2 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this Agreement or a specific SOW if the other party materially breaches the Agreement and fails to cure the breach within **15 days** of written notice.

### 14.3 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate an SOW for convenience with **30 days** written notice. Client remains responsible for payment of all fees for work completed or in progress through the termination date, including any non-cancellable third-party costs incurred on Client's behalf.

### 14.4 Effect of Termination

Upon termination: (a) all outstanding fees become immediately due; (b) each party returns or destroys the other's Confidential Information upon written request; (c) PanteraClaw delivers any completed Deliverables (payment must be current); (d) provisions that by nature survive (confidentiality, IP, payment, limitation of liability, indemnification, governing law) will survive.

---

## 15. Force Majeure

Neither party is liable for delays caused by circumstances beyond its reasonable control, including acts of God, natural disasters, pandemic, war, cyberattacks, government actions, third-party infrastructure failures, or internet outages. The affected party will notify the other promptly and resume performance as soon as reasonably possible.

---

## 16. Dispute Resolution

### 16.1 Informal Resolution

Both parties will attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation for **30 days** before initiating formal proceedings.

### 16.2 Mediation

If informal resolution fails, the parties will attempt non-binding mediation through a mutually agreed mediator before proceeding to arbitration.

### 16.3 Binding Arbitration

Unresolved disputes will be settled by binding arbitration under the AAA Commercial Arbitration Rules with one arbitrator in [Your City], Utah. The award is final and enforceable in any court of competent jurisdiction.

### 16.4 Equitable Relief

Either party may seek injunctive or other equitable relief in court without first going through arbitration for claims involving IP infringement, confidentiality breaches, or other irreparable harm.

### 16.5 Class Action Waiver

All disputes will be resolved on an individual basis. Neither party waives the right to a class action arbitration or lawsuit.

## 17. General Provisions

**Governing Law.** This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Utah, without regard to conflict of law principles.

**Entire Agreement.** This Agreement, together with all executed SOWs and Change Orders, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties regarding its subject matter and supersedes all prior discussions and representations.

**Amendment.** This Agreement may only be amended by a written instrument signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

**Severability.** If any provision is found unenforceable, it will be modified to the minimum extent necessary and the remaining provisions will continue in effect.

**No Waiver.** Failure to enforce any provision does not waive the right to enforce it later. Waivers must be in writing.

**Independent Contractors.** The parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, employment, or agency relationship.

**Assignment.** Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's prior written consent, except that PanteraClaw may assign in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets.

**Notices.** All formal notices must be in writing, sent by email with read receipt or delivery confirmation, or by certified mail to the contact information in the applicable SOW.

**Counterparts.** This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including electronic signatures, each of which is equally valid.
