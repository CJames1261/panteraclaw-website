# PanteraClaw — Master Services Agreement (MSA)

> **Status:** Draft — **This is not legal advice. Review and finalize with a licensed attorney in Utah before use, as laws vary by jurisdiction and specific circumstances.**
> **Version:** 1.1
> **Last Updated:** April 10, 2026

---

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between **Agentic AI LLC dba PanteraClaw**, a Utah limited liability company with its principal place of business in Utah ("**Service Provider**," "we," or "us") and the client identified in the applicable Statement of Work ("**Client**," "you," or "your"). This Agreement governs all professional services engagements between the parties, including but not limited to business web development, database creation, management, and optimization, dashboard and visualization development, agentic workflow design, and advanced analytics.

By signing a Statement of Work that references this Agreement, both parties agree to be bound by these terms.

---

## 1. Definitions

- "**Agreement**" means this Master Services Agreement and all Statements of Work, exhibits, and amendments incorporated herein.
- "**Statement of Work** (SOW)" means a written document executed by both parties describing the specific scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for a given engagement.
- "**Deliverables**" means the work product, code, models, documentation, reports, or other outputs PanteraClaw produces specifically for Client under an SOW.
- "**Background IP**" means any intellectual property, tools, frameworks, libraries, methodologies, or know-how owned or developed by PanteraClaw independently of any engagement.
- "**Client Data**" means any data, content, or materials provided by Client to PanteraClaw for use in performing services.
- "**Change Order**" means a written amendment to an SOW that modifies scope, timeline, or fees, signed by both parties.

---

## 2. Services

### 2.1 Statements of Work

All services are governed by individual Statements of Work. Each SOW must specify:

- Project scope and objectives
- Deliverables and acceptance criteria
- Project timeline and milestones
- Fees and payment schedule
- Any specific IP ownership terms that differ from Section 8 defaults

An SOW is binding only when signed by authorized representatives of both parties.

### 2.2 Service Standards

PanteraClaw will perform services **professionally and in a workmanlike manner**, consistent with industry standards applicable to the services described in the SOW. PanteraClaw does not guarantee specific business outcomes, results, or performance levels unless **explicitly defined as measurable technical specifications** in the SOW (e.g., system uptime percentages, specific model accuracy thresholds). No warranties are provided regarding AI model performance, agentic system behavior, or business impacts beyond those expressly stated in the SOW.

### 2.3 Subcontractors

PanteraClaw may engage subcontractors or independent contractors to assist in delivering services, provided PanteraClaw remains fully responsible for their work as if performed by PanteraClaw directly. PanteraClaw will ensure all subcontractors are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as those in Section 9 and comply with applicable laws.

---

## 3. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

- Designate an authorized project representative with decision-making authority.
- Provide accurate, complete, and timely information, data, feedback, and approvals required for service delivery.
- Ensure that any Client Data or materials provided to PanteraClaw do not infringe third-party rights, violate applicable laws (including data privacy laws such as CCPA, GDPR if applicable, or Utah Consumer Privacy Act), and are free of viruses or harmful code.
- Provide access to systems, infrastructure, or personnel reasonably necessary to perform the services.
- Review and approve deliverables within the timeframes specified in the SOW.

**Impact of Client Delays.** If Client fails to fulfill any of the above responsibilities, resulting in a delay in PanteraClaw's performance, the project timeline will be extended by an equivalent period, PanteraClaw will not be liable for such delays, and Client will reimburse PanteraClaw for any additional costs incurred. Such delays do not constitute a breach by PanteraClaw.

## 4. Revisions and Scope Changes

### 4.1 Included Revisions

Each SOW will specify the number of revision rounds included (e.g., up to 2 rounds of corrections). Revisions are limited to corrections of Deliverables that do not conform to the SOW specifications and exclude new features, scope expansions, design changes, or additional requirements.

### 4.2 Scope Changes

Any change to the agreed scope, deliverables, timeline, or fees requires a signed Change Order. PanteraClaw is not obligated to perform, and will not commence, work on out-of-scope requests until a Change Order is fully executed and any additional fees are paid.

### 4.3 Verbal Approvals

Verbal approvals, emails without mutual sign-off, or informal instructions do not modify this Agreement or any SOW. **All modifications, including scope changes or approvals, must be in a signed writing.**

---

## 5. Fees and Payment

### 5.1 Fees

Fees are as set forth in each SOW. PanteraClaw may adjust rates for new SOWs or renewals with 30 days' written notice.

### 5.2 Invoicing and Payment

- Invoices are due **net 30 days** from the invoice date unless otherwise specified in the SOW.
- Engagements exceeding $5,000 **will require** a deposit (typically 25–50%) prior to work commencement, as specified in the SOW.
- Milestone-based payments are due upon milestone completion and Client approval (or deemed approval if not disputed timely).

### 5.3 Late Payments

Overdue amounts accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (or 18% per annum) or the maximum rate permitted by Utah law, whichever is lower. PanteraClaw may, upon 5 days' notice: (i) suspend services or withhold Deliverables on accounts 15+ days past due; (ii) accelerate all unpaid fees; or (iii) terminate the Agreement without liability.

### 5.4 Disputed Invoices

Client must notify PanteraClaw in writing of any good-faith invoice dispute within **10 days** of invoice receipt, specifying the basis. Undisputed portions remain due on the original due date. Disputes do not excuse timely payment of undisputed amounts.

### 5.5 Expenses

Client will reimburse reasonable out-of-pocket expenses (e.g., third-party APIs, cloud hosting, travel) at cost plus **15%** administrative fee. Expenses exceeding $500 require Client's prior written approval.

### 5.6 Non-Refundable Fees; No Refunds

All fees for completed milestones or Deliverables are **non-refundable**, except in cases of PanteraClaw's material breach where Deliverables fail to meet SOW specifications after all included revisions.

### 5.7 Taxes

Client is responsible for all applicable sales, use, or other taxes on the services, excluding taxes on PanteraClaw's net income.

## 6. AI-Assisted Deliverables

Services may incorporate AI-generated content, machine learning models, or probabilistic systems. 

- **Disclosure.** PanteraClaw will disclose AI-assisted elements in the SOW or Deliverables documentation.
- **No Output Guarantees.** AI outputs are inherently variable and non-deterministic. PanteraClaw warrants only against explicitly defined SOW performance metrics (e.g., benchmark accuracy).
- **Client Obligations.** Client must independently review, test, validate, and approve all AI outputs for accuracy, suitability, bias, compliance, and fitness for purpose **before any use, deployment, or reliance**. Client assumes all risks of AI errors, hallucinations, or unintended behaviors.
- **Third-Party Dependencies.** Deliverables may rely on third-party AI services/APIs, which are subject to change. Client is responsible for ongoing costs and compatibility.
- **Compliance.** Client ensures AI use complies with applicable laws (e.g., bias regulations, data protection).

## 7. Agentic and Automated Systems

For agentic workflows, autonomous agents, or automated decision systems:

- PanteraClaw will provide documentation on intended behaviors, known limitations, risks (e.g., model drift, adversarial attacks), and recommended safeguards.
- **Client Safeguards Required.** Client must implement and maintain robust controls pre-deployment, including human oversight, logging, rate limiting, error handling, and emergency shutdowns.
- **Risk Allocation.** Upon SOW acceptance/Deliverable approval, **Client assumes all liability** for agentic system actions, decisions, errors, damages, or failures — including those from edge cases, integrations, or post-delivery changes.
- **Recommendation.** Client should conduct extensive testing, including red-teaming, in a non-production environment before deployment.

## 8. Intellectual Property

### 8.1 Background IP License

PanteraClaw retains sole ownership of all Background IP. PanteraClaw grants Client a **non-exclusive, perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide, royalty-free license** to use Background IP solely as embedded in Deliverables for Client's internal business purposes.

### 8.2 Custom Deliverables IP

- **Default:** PanteraClaw retains ownership of custom Deliverables; Client receives the same license as for Background IP (perpetual, non-exclusive for internal use).
- **Full Assignment:** Available only via explicit SOW term and full payment; assignment occurs post-final payment. "Work-for-hire" status requires clear SOW language.
- Client grants PanteraClaw a license to use Client Data solely for service delivery.

### 8.3 Client Materials

Client retains ownership of pre-existing Client Data/IP. Client warrants it does not infringe third-party rights.

### 8.4 Open Source

PanteraClaw will provide a list of material open-source components and applicable notices. **Client is solely responsible for ongoing open-source compliance.**

### 8.5 Marketing Rights

PanteraClaw may use Client's name/logo and anonymized project descriptions in portfolios/marketing, unless Client provides written opt-out notice.

## 9. Confidentiality

### 9.1 Obligations

Each party (the "Receiving Party") will hold the disclosing party's ("Disclosing Party") Confidential Information in strict confidence, using it only for purposes of this Agreement, and disclosing it only to need-to-know personnel bound by equivalent terms. Receiving Party will protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care as its own (but no less than reasonable care).

### 9.2 Confidential Information Defined

Non-public information marked confidential or reasonably understood as such from context, including Client Data, business plans, technical specs, pricing, source code, and methodologies. Excludes information that: (a) is publicly known without breach; (b) was known prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed; or (d) is rightfully received from a third party.

### 9.3 Duration

Obligations survive for **5 years** post-Agreement termination (perpetual for trade secrets).

### 9.4 Return/Destruction

Upon request or termination, return or destroy Confidential Information (certifying destruction).

## 13. Termination

### 13.1 Term

This MSA remains effective until terminated. SOWs complete upon delivery/payment.

### 13.2 Termination for Cause

### 13.3 Effects

Upon termination: (i) pay outstanding fees; (ii) cease using Confidential Information; (iii) return materials. Accrued rights survive.
