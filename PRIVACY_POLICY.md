PanteraClaw, doing business as Agentic AI LLC ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our website and services. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, how we use and share it, and what rights you have regarding that information.

- Prospective clients who contact us via forms, email, or other channels

If you are a California resident, please also review **Section 12 (California Privacy Rights — CCPA)**.

## 4. AI Processing and Third-Party AI Providers

**This section is particularly important for clients using our AI-assisted services, including agentic workflows.**

### 4.1 Use of Third-Party AI APIs

Some of our services, such as agentic workflow design and advanced analytics, are built on or integrate with third-party AI platforms including, but not limited to, large language model providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS). When you or your data interact with these systems:

- Data inputs may be transmitted to and processed by these third-party providers' systems.
- Each provider's own privacy policy and data processing terms govern how they handle data on their infrastructure.
- We select providers with commercially reasonable data protection practices and, where applicable, execute Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with them that include standard contractual clauses for international data transfers if needed.
- We take reasonable steps to minimize what data is sent to third-party AI systems — using anonymization, aggregation, or synthetic data substitution where feasible and as specified in the SOW.

### 4.2 What We Do Not Do

- We do not knowingly transmit regulated data (e.g., Protected Health Information (PHI), Payment Card Industry (PCI) data, government-issued ID numbers, or other special categories of data under GDPR) to third-party AI APIs without a written agreement explicitly covering that data class and implementing appropriate safeguards.
- We do not sell or share your AI interaction data with third parties for their own marketing or model-training purposes.
- We do not use data from one client's engagement to benefit another client without explicit consent.

### 4.3 Your Responsibility

Do not submit sensitive personal data, trade secrets, regulated information, or confidential business data to any AI-assisted tool or workflow unless the applicable SOW explicitly addresses that data class, the necessary safeguards (e.g., DPAs) are in place, and you have conducted your own risk assessment.

We implement technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards designed to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction, appropriate to the nature of the data and services provided. These include:

- Encryption in transit (TLS 1.3/HTTPS) and at rest for sensitive data

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information and meets applicable thresholds, we will notify affected individuals and relevant authorities without undue delay as required by law (e.g., within 72 hours under GDPR, 60 days under certain U.S. state laws).
