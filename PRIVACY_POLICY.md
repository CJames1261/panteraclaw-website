**This Policy applies to:**
- Visitors to our website at [yourdomain.com]
- Prospective clients who contact us via forms, email, or other channels
- Current clients receiving services under a signed agreement

**Two roles we play:**
- **Data Controller** — When we collect your information directly (e.g., contact form submissions, website analytics), we determine how that data is used. This Policy governs that processing.
- **Data Processor** — When we process data on behalf of a client as part of a contracted engagement, our handling of that data is governed by the applicable services agreement and any Data Processing Agreement (DPA) executed with that client, not this Policy.

If you are a California resident, please also review **Section 12 (California Privacy Rights — CCPA)**.
If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, please also review **Section 13 (EEA/UK — GDPR)**.

## 4. AI Processing and Third-Party AI Providers

**This section is particularly important for clients using our AI-assisted services.**

### 4.1 Use of Third-Party AI APIs

Some of our services are built on or integrate with third-party AI platforms including, but not limited to, large language model providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, AWS). When you or your data interact with these systems:

- Data inputs may be transmitted to and processed by these third-party providers' systems.
- Each provider's own privacy policy and data processing terms govern how they handle data on their infrastructure.
- We select providers with commercially reasonable data protection practices and, where applicable, execute Data Processing Agreements with them.
- We take reasonable steps to minimize what data is sent to third-party AI systems — using anonymization, aggregation, or synthetic data substitution where feasible.

### 4.2 What We Do Not Do

- We do not knowingly transmit regulated data (PHI, PCI, government-issued ID numbers) to third-party AI APIs without a written agreement explicitly covering that data class.
- We do not sell or share your AI interaction data with third parties for their own marketing or model-training purposes.
- We do not use data from one client's engagement to benefit another client.

### 4.3 Your Responsibility

Do not submit sensitive personal data, trade secrets, or regulated information to any AI-assisted tool or workflow unless the applicable SOW explicitly addresses that data class and the applicable safeguards are in place.

## 5. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

**Service providers.** We work with vendors who help us operate our business (e.g., cloud hosting providers, analytics tools, email platforms, payment processors, AI API providers). These vendors are contractually required to process data only on our behalf and in accordance with our instructions.

**Legal requirements.** We may disclose information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the safety of any person, prevent fraud, or enforce our legal rights.

**Business transfers.** If PanteraClaw is acquired, merges with another entity, or transfers substantially all of its assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you via website notice or direct communication before your data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

**With your consent.** We may share information with third parties when you have given us explicit permission to do so.

We do not share personal information with third parties for their own marketing purposes.
